My favorite tv shows of all time

My all-time comfort series.

Derry Girls

How can you not love it? This series hase 'show, dont't tell' down to a t, and oodles of humor to boot. Now, swear on Dolly you'll schedule a rewatch soon!

Arrested Development

It took me some time to get into this show, but my goodness is it great! Attention to detail, running gags, and way more depth than I initially thought. Favorite quote: "If that is a veiled criticism about me, I won't hear it and I won't respond to it." Words to live by, truly.

Schitt's Creek

Ew, David! What do you mean you don't like this show?! You cannot help fall in love with these characters. This series is extremely quotable (and memeable), but to name a few favorite quotes:
"Eat glass!"
"Lick rust!"
"I don't skate through life, David. I walk through life. In really nice shoes."
And, quite frankly, all of 'A little bit Alexis'.
